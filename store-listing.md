## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Never miss a word in your meetings again. Echobit is your AI-powered meeting companion that automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes every conversation — so you can focus on the meeting, not on taking notes.

**🎙️ Smart Recording**
Tap once to record. Echobit captures your meeting audio clearly with background noise awareness. Pause, resume, or stop anytime. Your recording is automatically saved and ready for transcription.

**📝 Instant AI Transcription**
Get accurate, word-for-word transcriptions powered by AI within seconds of finishing your recording. Works for interviews, lectures, client calls, team standups, and brainstorming sessions.

**🌐 15+ Indian Languages**
Echobit is built for India. Get transcriptions in:
• Hindi (हिन्दी)
• Malayalam (മലയാളം)
• Tamil (தமிழ்)
• Telugu (తెలుగు)
• Bengali (বাংলা)
• Kannada (ಕನ್ನಡ)
• Marathi (मराठी)
• Gujarati (ગુજરાતી)
• Punjabi (ਪੰਜਾਬੀ)
• English and more

**🤖 AI-Powered Summaries**
Don't have time to read the full transcript? Echobit generates a concise AI summary highlighting the key points discussed — in seconds.

**📋 Meeting Minutes & Action Items**
Automatically extract structured meeting minutes and action items from your recordings. Share them with your team instantly.

**📄 PDF Export**
Export your transcriptions, summaries, and meeting minutes as professional PDF documents to share with stakeholders.

**☁️ Cloud Sync**
Your recordings and transcriptions are safely backed up to the cloud. Access them from any device, anytime. Turn off cloud sync to keep everything local.

**🔒 Privacy First**
Your meetings are private. Recordings are encrypted and stored securely. You are always in control of your data.

**🆓 Free Plan Available**
Start free — no credit card required. Upgrade when you need more recordings, longer sessions, or advanced AI features.
